Thematic Watch
1. Nvidia’s $1T Inference Inflection. GTC 2026 marked a paradigm shift from training-centric to inference-centric AI economics. Jensen Huang declared the inflection point of inference has arrived and doubled his demand forecast to $1T through 2027. Vera Rubin’s 10x performance/watt advantage makes the unit economics of AI factories dramatically better. The Groq 3 LPU adds 35x inference throughput/watt — SemiAnalysis says Jensen was sandbagging and the real number is 50x. Hyperscaler capex is accelerating, not peaking. Watch: NVDA, MU, AVGO, ANET.
2. Oil Shock vs. Rate Policy Collision. WTI at $95 and Brent above $100 are importing inflation into the economy just as the Fed wants to pivot to cuts. Core PCE is already 3.1% YoY and February payrolls showed −92K jobs with unemployment at 4.4%. If Hormuz remains disrupted through Q2, the stagflation playbook (long energy, short consumer discretionary, overweight quality) becomes consensus. The EIA forecasts Brent above $95/bbl for the next two months before normalizing to ~$70 by year-end — if the conflict ends.
3. SEC Semi-Annual Reporting Push. The SEC’s proposal to make quarterly earnings optional could reshape market microstructure. Less frequent reporting reduces alpha opportunities for fundamental investors while potentially reducing short-termism. The EU and UK eliminated mandatory quarterlies roughly a decade ago — most companies continued reporting voluntarily. Audit firms, sell-side research, and data vendors face structural revenue risk. A proposal could come as soon as April with a 30-day comment period before a vote.
4. AI Agent Platform Wars Heat Up. OpenClaw’s explosive adoption has spawned a new competitive layer. Nvidia launched NemoClaw with enterprise security (OpenShell) at GTC; Jensen compared OpenClaw to Linux and HTML. Meta is cutting headcount while pouring $27B into Nebius AI compute. GPT-5.4 crossed $1B in annualized net-new revenue in its first week. Alibaba created a new Token Hub unit consolidating all AI products. The enterprise AI agent market is rapidly becoming the next trillion-dollar addressable opportunity.
