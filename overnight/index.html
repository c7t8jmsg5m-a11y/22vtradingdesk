Bottom Line
Qatar's energy minister warning of $150 oil in 2-3 weeks and predicting all Gulf exporters will declare force majeure is the overnight headline that reframes the risk calculus. Stay defensive on macro-sensitive names; the Kaabi interview changes the oil risk premium meaningfully to the upside.
