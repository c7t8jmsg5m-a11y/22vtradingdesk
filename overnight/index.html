Bottom Line
Overnight tone is cautiously constructive on the surface but fragile underneath. Reports that Iranian operatives have reached out to the US to explore peace talks provide a glimmer of an off-ramp, but the IRGC's "set ablaze" rhetoric undercuts diplomacy. With Costco, Marvell, and Kroger reporting after the close, plus weekly jobless claims this morning and Friday's NFP looming, lean into quality and maintain energy hedges into the weekend.
