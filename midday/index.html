Key Single-Stock Narratives

This comes days after the company wrote a private loan to Infinite Commerce Holdings down from 100 cents to zero in just three months. JP Morgan and Evercore both trimmed PTs. The stock is now RSI 26.7 — oversold, but the fundamental story is still deteriorating. Watch for second-order effects in CLO markets and bank lending to the alt manager complex. The read-through to ARES (−4.8%), BX (−3.3%), APO (−2.0%), KKR (−2.7%) is direct and unmistakable.

LTL tons per day were already declining 9% in Q3, and today's move suggests the market is repricing for an even worse Q1. Analyst consensus is a Hold with a $184 PT. The transportation sector is a canary: JBHT −5.4%, UPS −3.0%.
